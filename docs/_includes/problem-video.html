We are designing an assistive mobility device for Kinematic/Kinesthetic, a live performance by the “physically integrated” AXIS Dance Company. The goal is not to “fix” disability by imitating non-disabled movement, but to use engineered locomotion as an expressive medium—exploring unfamiliar, “species-atypical,” and/or “superhuman” movement that expands how audiences understand ability and embodiment.
