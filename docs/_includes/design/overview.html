Overview

Design page scope: final system architecture, trade choices, and subsystem relationships. Implementation details and test results are separated onto the Implementation and Performance pages to avoid repeating the same final-stack summary.

Mechanical concept: six Klann linkage leg modules, one independently phased servo/gearbox per leg.
Control concept: alternating tripod phase relationships, startup contact-pose sequencing, and body-velocity commands mapped to crank phase/RPM.
Design intent: expressive legged motion for performance while retaining enough determinism for navigation and rider-scale payloads.
Autonomy concept: RGB-D SLAM plus linkage-based odometry, fused through a planar ROS 2 frame tree.
Safety concept: precharge/contactor sequencing, shared servo enable, conservative speed limits, and operator override.
Stage concept: browser-based off-stage supervision with maps, waypoint goals, teleop, and software e-stop mode.
