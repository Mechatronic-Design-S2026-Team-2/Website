Design

This page documents the final class configuration: a six-actuator Klann-linkage hexapod with onboard Jetson perception/navigation, ESP32 power and motor control, RS485 servo drives, and a browser-accessible operator interface. Earlier pages described side-leg and coupled-shaft concepts; those have been superseded by the independently phased six-leg implementation.
